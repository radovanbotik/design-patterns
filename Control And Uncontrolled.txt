                Controlled and Uncontrolled Components
1.Uncontrolled Components:
    Components that keep track of their own states and release data
    only when some even occurs
    (e.g hitting submit button form)

2.Controlled Components:
    Components that do not keep track of their own states all state
    is passed in as props
    (e.g using useState hook with text inputs)

    --preffered type
